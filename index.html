About Us

02 We are a team of highly experienced trainers from both Industry and Academia with more than 10+ years of experience to help students to learn latest technologies , perform practical and work on real time projects on latest technologies.

03 Besides skill building for graduates, we also do provide IT subject classes to academic students.
